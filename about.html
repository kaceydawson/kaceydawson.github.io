Our Founder

The roots of our firm began over 28 years ago when our founder, Robert Hockett, began his career in Banking, where he collaborated exclusively with Licensed Professionals for a Fortune 50 Bank. A few years later, after learning what makes a professional practice financially successful (from the lender side), he received his Certified Financial Planner (CFPâ„¢) designation, and founded Cambridge Wealth Counsel, a fee-only wealth management firm. Cambridge successfully combined family wealth management with business consulting/coaching for seasoned licensed professionals and successful business owners.

In 2017, Cambridge merged with Modera Wealth Management ($13 Billion UA currently), where Robert was a Principal and member of the Board of Directors. In 2021, Robert, with his wife Maria, took a two-year sabbatical to serve a church service mission in Milan, Italy during Covid. Upon returning from service in Italy, Robert launched ThePracticeConsultant.com to exclusively provide Practice Consulting and Loan Advisory Services for Licensed Professionals.

He is an affiliate member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Financial Planning Association.
